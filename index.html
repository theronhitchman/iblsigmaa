IBL SIGMAA

An effort is under way to form a Special Interest Group of the Mathematical Association of America, or SIGMAA, which focuses on inquiry-based learning (IBL).

Some Basic Information

As of July 30, 2015, over 200 MAA members have expressed interest in being a charter member of the IBL SIGMAA. Please let us know if you are interested.
